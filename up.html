Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh , formerly United Provinces, State, north-central India. Area: 93,933 sq mi (243,286 sq km). Population: (2011) 199,581,477. Capital: Lucknow. It is bordered by Nepal; the states of Bihar, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Haryana, and Uttarakhand; and Delhi national capital territory. The state is the most populous in the country
